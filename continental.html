Continental Confluence Dashboard

Problem Statement

Design an admin dashboard to keep track of Confluence's usage within Continental Automotive Singapore

Scroll Down

Overview & Background

Confluence is a content collaboration software/ social media for corporates

Continental recently adopted Confluence as their main tool for project collaboration, event notifications and content sharing. As such, the upper management would like to keep track of how popular Confluence really is.

Solution

A handy dashboard that displays various information regarding the recent usage of Confluence.

These information include top liked pages, top contributors, pages that use their own styling and scripts, and so on.

Survey Analysis

Being an intern in the company for merely 2 weeks, I roughly had an idea of how the process of unit testing is done, from the requesting, procuring of different units, running the actual test and reporting test results. However, I would still need additional input from team members regarding the requesting procedures.

I emailed my colleagues a google survey that I created and received a total of 14 responses along with some valuable insights:

  • Requesting tasks through emails is time-consuming: An overwhelming 71.4% of my team members spend more than 10 minutes on requesting 1 task
  • There is an even distribution of An overwhelming 85.7% of my team members find the current method difficult
